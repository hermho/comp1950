The Design

Ideas

Our reimagination of the course website focuses on student experience, visual appeal and overall usability. There are a number of additions and changes implemented to overhaul the website. The result is contemporary and refined.

Design

Outside the conventional course format, our proposed design follows a simplified layout that presents contents in an orderly arrangement. The overall design is clean and refined. The colour scheme of blue, teal and lime give the website a fresh and contemporary appeal.

Links

Navigation is centralized into one menu at the upper left corner. Links are no longer occupying valuable real estate on your screen. The collapsible menu enables toggling between viewing page contents and navigation links.

Styles

Using a cohesive colour palette, the styling for photos, headings and links are exaggerated for greater emphasis in information importance. For lecture pages, students are given stylesheet options to view contents in standard white text on black background or in inverted colour scheme.

Isotope

Instead of burying the course contents, all of the information are presented and organized right at the home page. Students can filter them using Isotope, a jQuery plugin, based on the content type: lectures, exercises, homework or projects.

Codepen

The application of Codepen in the lecture pages promotes greater engagement with the course material. Rather than flipping between browser and text editors, the live code environment allows students to edit and preview HTML, CSS and JavaScript directly on the page.
